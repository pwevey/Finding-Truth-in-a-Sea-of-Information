This project is a growing collection of reflections on Artificial Intelligence, Large Language Models, and the nature of truth — examined through a Christian theological and technical lens.

As AI systems become increasingly fluent and persuasive, clarity is needed about what these systems are, what they are not, and where truth is actually grounded. This repository provides that clarity.
